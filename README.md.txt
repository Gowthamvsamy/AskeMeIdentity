# Aske Me Identity

Welcome to the Aske Me Identity Website repository! This document provides an overview of the project, including setup instructions and other relevant information.

## Overview

The Coaching Center Website is designed to offer information about our coaching services, including course details, schedules, and contact details. It aims to provide a user-friendly interface for students and parents to learn more about our offerings and get in touch with us.

## Features

- **Course Information**: Detailed descriptions of the courses we offer.
- **Schedule**: Up-to-date class schedules and important dates.
- **Faculty**: Information about our experienced instructors.
- **Contact Form**: Easy way for prospective students and parents to reach out.
- **Responsive Design**: Accessible on both desktop and mobile devices.
